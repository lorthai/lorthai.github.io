Thai's GitHub Projects

PacMen Generator

A project dedicated to generating any number of PacMan with a click of a button. These PacMen can be animated to bounce around the browser screen with other click of a button.

Eye Exercise

A project used to portray the tracking of your mouse cursor using a pair of eyes. The eyes will follow your cursor around your screen in real time.

Real Time Bus Tracker

A project that displays a bus route on a real map. With a click of a button, a marker will pinpoint the bus route stops from start to finish.
